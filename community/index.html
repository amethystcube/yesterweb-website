The Yesterweb Community

Here are some places you can participate in the Yesterweb community:

Discord

An internet-focused chat server with topic channels.
Why Discord?

Forum

An old-school-style forum for longer discussions.

Fediverse

A Mastodon instance on the fediverse (it's similar to Twitter)

Yesterweb Cafe & City

A 3D space for Yesterpunks created by with Mozilla Hubs.

Radio

A radio that plays independent music and user-submitted talkshows.

Zine

A digital zine made up of user-submissions.

Link Directory

A moderated & tagged directory of user-submitted links to fun and interesting websites.

gemini://

A server on the gemini:// protocol that offers the ability to make your own page. A special browser is required to view our page at: gemini://cities.yesterweb.org.

Minecraft Server

A Minecraft server running v1.17.1 vanilla. Join in your Minecraft client at minecraft.yesterweb.org
