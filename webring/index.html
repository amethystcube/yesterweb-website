Welcome to the Yesterweb Webring!

Please send any questions you have to: contact@yesterweb.org

This webring is for anyone who is tired of how boring and same-y the internet is today. It's for anyone who is sick of seeing websites used purely to drive monetization, informative blogs that ask you to subscribe to see content.

If you believe that the internet is being wrongly controlled by capitalism, or that social interaction online should be more than it currently is on Facebook, Twitter, or Instagram, then you belong here.

Webring Rules

  • No hate speech or discriminatory content.
  • No professional portfolios/resumes.
  • No third-party advertisements.

Your site will be removed from the webring if:

  • Your website remains inactive for an extended period of time.
  • Your website contains excessive sensitive/NSFW content without a content warning.
  • Your website link breaks for an extended period of time.

FAQ

What is a webring? A webring is a collection of links pointing to different websites. A webring is usually centered around a common theme. Think of it as a way to 'put your site out there' without the need for search engines.

How to style/unstyle the Widget

Before styling your website, we recommend unstyling it first. To display an unstyled widget, just add the class "yw-raw" to the class of your widget. You can see a live example on this CodePen.

If you are using the full widget, your code will look like: 

class="yw-widget-full yw-raw"

Once it has been unstyled, you can use the original widget class (in this case yw-widget-full) to style your widget.
